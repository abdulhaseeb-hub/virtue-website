Our story

Fidelen is a Saudi B2B marketplace, owned by Smart Applications Digital Marketing Co. Ltd.

A single go-to for your business needs to succeed in one platform, from business services like financial services, marketing, cleaning and even shipping services, to customizable products that are made on demand such as restaurant furniture, retail store decoration, and installation of devices to the staff uniform.. and more  And, when you accept a quotation and pay with Fidelen, you’re covered by our Deal Agreement. We’ll hold your full purchase price, and pay it in 3 phases once you are happy with the deliverables. 

The founders started this business back in 2020 after they faced many challenges during their +15 years of the entrepreneurial journey, the difficulty of finding a trustworthy service provider that was committed to quality, and increasing their business exposure to new customers.
